# Billing and Invoices

## Accepted payment methods
We support major cards and ACH bank transfer for annual plans.
Prepaid gift cards are not supported for subscription renewals.

## Failed payment policy
If a renewal charge fails, we retry automatically after 3 days and again after 7 days.
If all retries fail, the account is moved to read-only mode until payment is updated.

## Downloading invoices
Workspace owners can download PDF invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoices.
Invoices are generated on the billing date and include tax details.
