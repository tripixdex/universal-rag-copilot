# Returns and Refunds

## Return window
Customers can return most physical products within 30 days of delivery.
Items marked as final sale are not eligible for return.

## Refund timeline
After we receive and inspect a return, we issue a refund within 2 business days.
Refunds to cards usually settle in 5-10 business days.
Refunds to bank transfers usually settle in 1-3 business days.

## Shipping fees
Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged.
